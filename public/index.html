Questions

Frequently asked


Is this legal advice?
No. LeaseCheck is an informational tool that analyses your lease against publicly available UK law. It is not a substitute for advice from a qualified solicitor. For complex situations, contact Shelter or a housing solicitor.
Does the Renters' Rights Act affect my existing lease?
Yes. The Act came into force on 1 May 2026. Certain provisions — including the abolition of Section 21 — now apply to all existing tenancies, not just new ones. Upload your current lease to find out what has changed for you.
Is my PDF stored or shared?
No. Your PDF is sent directly to the analysis engine and is never stored, logged, or shared. We do not retain any copy of your document after the report is generated.
What types of tenancy does this cover?
LeaseCheck covers private rented sector agreements in England: ASTs (signed before May 2026), new periodic tenancies, and renewal letters. It does not currently cover Scotland, Wales, or social housing.
Can I get a refund if I'm not happy?
Yes. If your report fails to generate or you feel it hasn't analysed your document correctly, contact us within 24 hours for a full refund.
